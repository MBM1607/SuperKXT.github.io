SchoolNexus

February 2017

SchoolNexus is a School Management and Automation System.
I was tasked with creating the entire system from scratch. I created everything from graphic assets, to the user-interface, the program logic, and the database schema.
I started with the logo design. I was given no specific requirements about the logo, only that it should look good.
I tried to convey the idea of the product in a visually pleasing manner with the logo.
The logo is made up of a group of pencils around a cube. The pencils obviously represent the academic nature of the product. The multitude of pencils represent the different aspects of the schools management the application planned to cover. The cube or package alludes to the product being a software application. The logo as a whole loosely resemebles a flower.
I settled on green as the primary color of the product.
